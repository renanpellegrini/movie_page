Daisy Jones and The Six

Synopsis

In 1977, Daisy Jones & The Six were at their peak. Led by the explosive Daisy Jones and Billy Dunne, the band emerged from obscurity and became rich and famous. Then, after a sold-out show in Chicago, the group abruptly disbanded. Now, decades later, the members have finally agreed to come clean and reveal the truth. This is the story of how a rock band imploded at the height of their career.
